Dermal Fillers
Loss of volume in the face is one of the key culprits of ageing. As we grow older, our facial muscles and connecting tissue begin to sag, often resulting in sunken cheeks and temples.
Dermal fillers, are used effectively to lift and plump hollowness in the cheekbones and in the temples as well as to provide volume to the overall face.
Hyaluronic acid, which is found naturally in the body, is synthetically produced as a pure product and used as a filling agent for various parts of the face. Our preferred dermal filler is the world-renowned Juvederm.
Benefits of Dermal Fillers?
Dermal fillers are a non-invasive, non-surgical way to create a youthful appearance by lifting and plumping your cheeks, lips and jawline.
They effectively reduce wrinkles and folds, the most-common signs of ageing, and can make you look younger.
How long do dermal fillers last?
While dermal fillers aren’t permanent, they can last anywhere between six and 18 months – though this is dependent on the type of filler and where it’s used.
We used Juvederm, which typically lasts for around 12 months.
After procedure what to expect?
  • Redness in the area
  • You may experience slight bruising
  • There may be some slight swelling
  • The area may feel tender or sore to touch
